Publish with confidence

Geneious is the only bioinformatics software that lets you align sequences, edit your alignments, and build beautiful phylogenetic trees, using peer-reviewed algorithms (PauP*, Mrbayes and PhyMl), all in one streamlined interface.

Submit your sequences directly to Genbank with the Genbank Submission Plugin.

Don’t think about file formats ever again

Importing your data from legacy software is as easy as drag and drop. You can work with multiple file types concurrently without having to worry about conversion.

NGS Analysis

Map or assemble large raw data files from Illumina, 454, PacBio, Ion torrent or Ion Proton.

Identify novel or pathogenic variants in mapped contigs, or predict genes on de novo assembled genomes or large contigs using Glimmer, without ever leaving the software.

Elegant Primer Design

Manually or automatically design primers and probes, and test existing primers against new sequences – save on ordering new primers.

Design degenerate primers directly on alignments, assemblies or ambiguous sequences, and automatically trim primer sequences from your sequencing results.

Better science faster

Trusted, peer-reviewed bioinformatics algorithms, clear workflows, and beautiful presentation mean you get your analysis done faster, with more presentable results, and with less reliance on the busy bioinformatics guy down the corridor.

Align whole genomes with mauVe, with clear visualisation.
